How to change Screen Saver/wallpaper on Quantum X Prox?

Following the below steps should allow you to easily change the screen saver or wallpaper on your Quantum X Pro device.

  1. Unlock your Quantum X Pro and go to the home screen.
  2. Find and tap on the "Settings" app (gear icon).
  3. Scroll down and tap on "Display" or "Wallpaper & Display".
  4. Look for options related to "Wallpaper" or "Screen Saver".
  5. Tap on "Choose a new wallpaper" or "Set screen saver".
  6. Select a pre-installed wallpaper or tap "Gallery" to choose from your photos.
  7. Adjust the image position or crop if necessary.
  8. Tap "Set" or "Apply" to confirm and set the new screen saver.
  9. Exit the settings and return to the home screen to see the changes.
